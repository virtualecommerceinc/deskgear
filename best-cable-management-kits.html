FAQ

Are adhesive clips reliable long-term?

They can be for lighter cables on clean surfaces; heavier routes should use trays/channels.

How do I hide power bricks?

Use under-desk trays or baskets and leave airflow space around high-heat adapters.

Can cable cleanup improve productivity?

Yes. Cleaner routing reduces friction, makes devices easier to manage, and improves focus.
