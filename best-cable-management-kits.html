FAQ

What should I buy first for cable cleanup?

Start with an under-desk tray plus reusable ties. That solves most visible cable clutter quickly.

Are adhesive clips reliable long term?

They work best on clean, smooth surfaces and for lighter cables. Heavier loads should use trays or mounted channels.

How do I keep power bricks from hanging?

Use a mounted basket or under-desk tray and group adapters by device to reduce tangles and desk drag.
