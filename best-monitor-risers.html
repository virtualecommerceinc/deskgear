FAQ

Do monitor risers really improve posture?

They can help when the screen sits too low. Better monitor height often reduces neck tilt during long sessions.

What size riser should I get?

Choose a riser that fits your monitor base width and leaves room for keyboard/storage flow under the stand.

Are budget risers worth it?

Yes, if they are stable and correctly sized. Stability and proper height matter more than premium materials.
