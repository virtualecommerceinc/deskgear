How to choose the right riser

  1. Measure your monitor base width and desk depth first.
  2. Choose target screen height so the top third is near eye level.
  3. Decide if you need storage clearance under the riser for keyboard or accessories.

FAQ

Do monitor risers really improve posture?

They often help when monitors sit too low, reducing neck tilt during long work sessions.

Are budget risers worth it?

Yes—if stable and sized correctly. Stability and proper height matter more than premium materials.

Can I use a riser with dual monitors?

Yes, but verify width and load capacity. Many dual setups work better with two smaller risers or a wide platform.
