FAQ

Is a monitor light bar better for small desks?

Often yes, because it uses minimal desk surface while providing direct task illumination near the monitor.

When is a desk lamp the better choice?

A desk lamp is better when you need flexible angle control for writing, drawing, or off-screen task lighting.

Can I use both lamp and light bar?

Yes. Many setups use a light bar for screen work and a lamp for side-task zones.
