FAQ

Can I use both lamp and light bar?

Yes, many desk setups benefit from both when tasks vary across screen work and off-screen work.

Will a light bar reduce eye strain?

It can improve comfort by balancing contrast around screen-focused work, especially in dim rooms.

Does lamp color temperature matter?

Yes. Neutral/cool tones may help focus; warmer tones may feel better for evening use.
